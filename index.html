March 21, 2026 • Post-First Round Edition • 32 of 63 Games Played
The first round is in the books. Every team has played. Every roster has taken damage. And the picture is finally clear enough to start separating the contenders from the pretenders. Here's the thing about FDM: 32 of 63 games are done, but only 304 of a typical 783 total points have been scored. That's less than 40%. The remaining 31 games will generate roughly 480 more points — meaning the lion's share of FDM is still ahead of us. You can't win FDM in the first round, but you can lose it. The second round starts today.
